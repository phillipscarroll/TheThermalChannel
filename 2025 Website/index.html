The Thermal Channel
Independent Thermal Testing

Since 2023, this project has focused on benchmarking thermal interfaces and conducting tests of interest, with most content featured on our YouTube channel.

We strive for consistent, unbiased, real-world, and independent testing, though it's important to note that thermal paste isn't a critical factor in most scenarios.

In short, thermal interfaces are crucial for direct-die applications but have less impact in indirect applications.

All computers, GPUs, tools, and most thermal materials are funded personally. When companies provide thermal paste for testing, it is disclosed in the benchmark video.
